```text
Make two variants: (A) Night UI and (B) Tabletop Daylight.
Must be original; do not copy any existing board game’s trademarked logos/characters/layouts.
No watermarks, signatures, or model marks.
No text unless explicitly requested.
Keep shapes readable at small sizes; strong silhouette; minimal clutter.

Output: 3000x1500 transparent PNG.

Create THREE distinct wordmark concepts for the text: [GAME NAME]
Style constraints:
- Original typography (no copying famous fonts).
- Legible at small sizes.
- Slight tabletop flair (carved wood, stamp ink, or wax seal feel) but still modern.

Variant A (Night UI): cool highlights, cyan/lime accent options.
Variant B (Tabletop Daylight): warm inks, parchment/wood feel.
```
