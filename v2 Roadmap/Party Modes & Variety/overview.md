# Party Modes & Variety — Overview

## Goals
- Keep sessions fresh with curated scenarios (Jackbox-style “pick a mode and go”).
- Add optional party-safe variants that don’t require moderation.

## Dependencies
- Existing house rules/settings system.
- Show & Pacing moment hooks for variant callouts (nice-to-have).

## Phases
- Phase 1 (P1): Scenario presets v2 (mode + board + theme + VP targets)
- Phase 2 (P2): Party variants (event deck, speed rounds, chaos toggles)
